Introduction

Stephen R. Covey's book "The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" inspired this project. The aim is to create a professional web presence that showcases one's skills, accomplishments, and knowledge to enhance career prospects and professional development.

Task 1: Platform Choice and Rationale

Platform Chosen: Weebly

Rationale for Choice:

  • Ease of Use: Weebly's user-friendly design allows users to build websites quickly and easily, making it an excellent choice for those with limited technical expertise.
  • Customization and Flexibility: Weebly offers a range of themes and features that can be customized using drag-and-drop functionality, providing a high level of customization without coding.
  • Support and Resources: Weebly provides comprehensive support including tutorials, forums, and customer service, facilitating issue resolution and feature addition.
  • Proactivity in Skill Development: Weebly's platform encourages users to learn about search engine optimization (SEO), site analytics, and design optimization.
  • Futureproofing and Scalability: Weebly helps manage scalable content, allowing for the addition and modification of site elements as requirements evolve.

Task 2: Project Plan

Goals:

  • Create a professional website to showcase your work, abilities, and projects.
  • Encourage relationships with industry peers, potential career opportunities, and academic institutions.
  • Demonstrate a high level of knowledge in digital marketing and website development.

Target Audience:

  • Recruiters and Potential Employers: Professionals in the IT field are looking for individuals with impressive portfolios and relevant expertise.
  • Academic Institutions: Evaluators at universities review individuals' work and accomplishments for academic qualifications.
  • Professional Peers and Colleagues: Industry peers network and collaborate with one another.

Critical Sections of the Website:

  • Home: An introduction page outlining the individual's professional background and career aspirations.
  • About: Detailed information about educational background, professional history, and personal interests. Includes a professional photo and a downloadable CV.
  • Portfolio: A curated collection of noteworthy projects, including detailed descriptions, images, and links to repositories (such as GitHub) or live demonstrations.
  • Contact: An online form for direct communication, including links to professional networks such as LinkedIn and GitHub, as well as a business email address.
  • Resources & Learning: A section offering resources for professional growth, including guides, articles, and tutorials.

Content to Showcase:

  • Skills and Technologies: A comprehensive list of technical skills, including Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Django, and SQL.
  • Projects: Detailed accounts of major projects, including online apps, data analytics jobs, and machine learning models, with photos and descriptions.
  • Achievements: A list of accolades, certifications, and honors, including academic awards and professional certifications.
  • Testimonials: Quotes from colleagues, mentors, or clients demonstrating professional and personal skills.
